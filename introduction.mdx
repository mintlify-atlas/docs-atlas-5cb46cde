## What is MinistryHub?

MinistryHub is a comprehensive church management SaaS platform built to scale from local congregations to global multi-site organizations. Instead of a one-size-fits-all approach, MinistryHub uses a **Multi-Hub Architecture** that gives each team the right tool for their specific needs.

## Key Features

### Multi-Tenant Church Management

Manage multiple congregations or campuses from a single account. Switch seamlessly between churches with context-aware permissions.

<Info>
Users with **Master** role can access and supervise all churches in the system without restrictions.
</Info>

### Worship & Music Ministry

- **ChordPro Format**: Full support for chord sheets with automatic transposition
- **Setlist Builder**: Organize songs for specific meetings with detailed assignments
- **Moderation Flow**: Collaborators propose edits that leaders approve before going live
- **Multi-Language Support**: Interface available in Spanish, English, and Portuguese

<Tip>
The ChordPro engine allows worship leaders to change song keys on the fly, automatically adjusting all chords.
</Tip>

### People & Team Management

- **Member Directory**: Centralized database with detailed profiles and role assignments
- **Areas & Teams**: Hierarchical organization (e.g., Multimedia Area → Video Team)
- **Invitation System**: Secure invite flow with customizable email templates
- **Permissions Management**: Role-based access control (RBAC) per church

### Service Tracking (Ushers Module)

- **Attendance Counting**: Track adults and children per meeting
- **Visitor Registration**: Dedicated workflow for first-time guests
- **Follow-up Tools**: Capture visitor information for ministry outreach

## Who Uses MinistryHub?

### Church Administrators

Manage your entire church operation from one platform:
- Oversee all ministries and departments
- Generate reports on attendance and engagement
- Control user permissions and access
- Configure multiple campuses or congregations

### Worship Leaders

Build and manage your music ministry:
- Maintain a digital song library with chord sheets
- Create setlists for services
- Assign musicians and vocalists
- Transpose songs to match your team's range

### Team Leaders

Coordinate your ministry area:
- Track team member participation
- Schedule services and assignments
- Communicate with your team
- View ministry-specific reports

### Master Administrators

For multi-site churches or denominational oversight:
- Access all churches in the system
- Perform global maintenance and updates
- Conduct cross-church audits
- Manage system-wide configurations

## Multi-Language Support

MinistryHub provides complete internationalization with support for:

- **Español** (Spanish)
- **English**
- **Português** (Portuguese)

<Note>
All interface strings are managed through a JSON key system, making it easy to add new languages or update translations remotely.
</Note>
